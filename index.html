WHAT WE ARE?

CartAR uses Augmented reality technology that allows retailers to provide a more immersive experience. It gives customers the ability to view products from their home environment before making a purchase. They can get information about them instantly- without having to leave home or even pick up the product. They can virtually try on items, feel the fabric of an item, or explore the features of a product before purchasing it. This would be especially beneficial for furniture, appliances and home improvement purchases, allowing customers to get a better understanding of how a product would look in their home. Augmented Reality could also be used to provide customers with a more personalized shopping experience, allowing them to customize their purchases based on their own preferences. Created by Sriram Mohan, Jaganath Nilesh Sai, Santhosh Kumar Rajesh in december 2022.
